## What is GitMesh CE?

GitMesh CE is an open-source platform designed to help development teams:

- **Correlate market signals** with engineering telemetry
- **Auto-generate ranked backlogs** based on data-driven insights
- **Create sprint plans** that align with business objectives
- **Route work efficiently** across your development stack

## Key Features

### Market Signal Integration
Connect various market data sources to understand customer needs and market trends.

### Engineering Telemetry
Integrate with your existing development tools to gather engineering metrics and insights.

### Automated Planning
Let GitMesh CE generate prioritized backlogs and sprint plans based on combined market and engineering data.

### Full Stack Sync
Keep all your development tools synchronized with the latest plans and priorities.
